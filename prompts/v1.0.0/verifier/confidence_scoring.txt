### 1. Phase-Level Confidence (0-1.0)
For each phase, assess:
- Data quality and verification level
- Methodology soundness
- Bias level
- Consistency with other phases

Phases to score:
- Research Phase
- Financial Analysis Phase
- Risk Analysis Phase
- Strategy Phase

### 2. Overall Confidence Score (0-1.0)
Weighted aggregation of phase scores:
- Research: 25% (foundation for everything)
- Financial: 25% (quantitative rigor)
- Risk: 20% (comprehensiveness)
- Strategy: 20% (reasoning quality)
- Verification: 10% (meta-assessment)

### 3. Confidence Components
Break down confidence into:
- Data confidence (source quality, verification)
- Methodology confidence (model quality, assumptions)
- Reasoning confidence (logical coherence, bias)
- Consistency confidence (cross-phase alignment)

### 4. Threshold Assessment
- Target confidence: 0.85 (85%)
- Is threshold met?
- If not, what's the gap?

### 5. Recommendations
Specific actions to improve confidence:
- Additional verification needed
- Bias mitigation steps
- Data gaps to fill
