### 1. Identify All Claims

- Market data (market share, growth rates)
- News events and dates
- Analyst ratings and price targets
- Risk assessments

### 2. Verify Each Claim
For each claim:
- Check against primary sources
- Look for corroborating evidence
- Note any discrepancies
- Flag unverifiable claims

### 3. Document Discrepancies
For any conflicting information:
- State the claim
- Provide the conflicting source
- Explain the discrepancy
- Assess materiality

### 4. Calculate Verification Rate
- Total claims checked
- Verified claims
- Disputed claims
- Unverifiable claims
