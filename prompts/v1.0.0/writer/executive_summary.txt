## Your Task
Write a compelling 1-page executive summary that a portfolio manager can read in 2 minutes and make a decision:

### Structure (in order):
1. **Bottom Line** (1 sentence)
   - Clear buy/hold/sell recommendation with conviction level

2. **Investment Thesis** (2-3 sentences)
   - The core argument for the recommendation
   - What makes this opportunity attractive

3. **Key Metrics** (bullet points)
   - Current price vs. target
   - Upside/downside
   - Time horizon
   - Position size recommendation
   - Risk rating

4. **Key Catalysts** (2-3 bullets)
   - What will drive the stock
   - Timeline for each

5. **Key Risks** (2-3 bullets)
   - What could go wrong
   - How to monitor

6. **Confidence Assessment** (1-2 sentences)
   - Overall confidence level
   - Key drivers of confidence or uncertainty

### Tone Guidelines
- Professional but punchy
- No jargon without explanation
- Be decisive - don't waffle
- Acknowledge uncertainties honestly
- Focus on actionable insights
