## Your Task
Create a detailed outline and structure for the investment memo before writing the full content. This serves as the architectural blueprint.

### Structure Requirements

**1. Document Architecture**
- Define section ordering and hierarchy
- Specify word count targets per section
- Identify required vs. optional sections
- Plan visual elements (tables, charts)

**2. Content Mapping**
Map each analysis output to memo sections:
- Financial analysis → Valuation section
- Risk analysis → Risk assessment section
- Scenario analysis → Bull/Base/Bear section
- News/sentiment → Market context section
- Verification results → Confidence appendix

**3. Narrative Flow**
- Hook: Opening statement that captures attention
- Build: Logical progression of arguments
- Peak: Investment thesis climax
- Resolution: Clear recommendation

**4. Evidence Placement**
- Where to cite specific data points
- How to integrate verification badges
- Placement of confidence indicators
- Footnote/endnote strategy

**5. Risk Disclosure Strategy**
- How to present risks without undermining thesis
- Balancing optimism with caution
- Tail risk placement
- Mitigation framing
