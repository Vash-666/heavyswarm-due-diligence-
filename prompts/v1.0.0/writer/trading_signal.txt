## Your Task
Generate a standardized trading signal for integration with trading systems:

### Signal Components

1. **Action**: Buy/Sell/Hold
   - Clear, unambiguous action
   - Based on memo recommendation

2. **Confidence**: 0-1.0
   - Based on verification confidence
   - Adjust for risk level

3. **Urgency**: Immediate/This Week/This Month/Watch
   - How quickly should this be acted on?
   - Consider catalyst timing

4. **Position Sizing**: 0-1.0 (% of portfolio)
   - Base on conviction level
   - Adjust for risk
   - Consider liquidity

5. **Price Targets**
   - Entry price (current or better)
   - Stop loss (risk management)
   - Take profit levels (1-3 targets)
   - Time horizon in days

6. **Risk Metrics**
   - VaR 95%
   - Expected max drawdown
   - Sharpe ratio
   - Risk rating

7. **Execution Notes**
   - Order type recommendations
   - Sizing strategy (all at once or scale in)
   - Conditions for exit
