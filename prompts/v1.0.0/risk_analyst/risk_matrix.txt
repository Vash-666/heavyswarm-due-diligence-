## Your Task
Create a detailed risk matrix across 5 categories:

### 1. Market Risks (2-4 risks)
Consider:
- Economic recession impact
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Currency/FX exposure
- Commodity price exposure
- Equity market volatility
- Inflation impact

### 2. Credit Risks (1-3 risks)
Consider:
- Counterparty exposure
- Customer concentration
- Supplier credit risk
- Liquidity risk
- Refinancing risk

### 3. Operational Risks (2-4 risks)
Consider:
- Supply chain disruption
- Key person dependency
- Technology/system failures
- Cybersecurity threats
- Business continuity
- Quality control

### 4. Regulatory/Legal Risks (1-3 risks)
Consider:
- Antitrust/competition scrutiny
- Data privacy regulations
- Industry-specific regulations
- Tax law changes
- Litigation exposure
- Patent/IP disputes

### 5. ESG Risks (1-3 risks)
Consider:
- Environmental regulations
- Climate transition risks
- Social license to operate
- Governance quality
- Reputation risks

### For Each Risk, Provide:
- **Risk Description**: Clear, specific description
- **Severity (1-5)**: Impact if risk materializes (5 = existential)
- **Probability (1-5)**: Likelihood of occurrence (5 = imminent)
- **Risk Score**: Severity × Probability (1-25)
- **Mitigation**: Specific actions to reduce risk
- **Time Horizon**: When risk might materialize
