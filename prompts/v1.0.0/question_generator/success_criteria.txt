Define Success Criteria for {{ticker}} Due Diligence:

## Investment Context
Ticker: {{ticker}}
Thesis: {{thesis}}
Time Horizon: {{time_horizon}}
Risk Tolerance: {{risk_tolerance}}
Position Size: {{position_size}}%
Priority: {{priority}}

## Your Task
Establish clear, measurable success criteria that will determine whether the due diligence process has produced a high-quality, actionable investment recommendation.

### 1. Data Quality Criteria

**Completeness Standards**
- Minimum 95% of required data points obtained
- All critical metrics (revenue, margins, cash flow) verified
- At least 3 independent sources for key claims
- No single-source dependencies for thesis-critical data

**Accuracy Standards**
- All financial data cross-referenced with primary sources
- Calculations verified with reverse-engineering
- Date stamps on all data points
- Provenance trail for every major claim

**Timeliness Standards**
- Financial data within 45 days of latest quarter
- News and sentiment within 7 days
- Market data real-time or same-day
- Analyst estimates current within 30 days

### 2. Analysis Quality Criteria

**Valuation Rigor**
- At least 2 valuation methodologies applied
- Sensitivity analysis on key assumptions
- Clear documentation of assumption sources
- Margin of safety calculation for entry price

**Risk Assessment Depth**
- Minimum 5 distinct risks identified
- Each risk has probability and impact assessment
- Mitigation strategies for top 3 risks
- Tail risk scenarios considered

**Scenario Robustness**
- Bull/Base/Bear scenarios defined
- Probability sum equals 100% (±5%)
- Each scenario has clear trigger conditions
- Expected value calculation across scenarios

### 3. Confidence Thresholds

**Overall Confidence Score**
- Critical priority: >90% required
- High priority: >85% required
- Medium priority: >80% required
- Low priority: >75% required

**Component Confidence Minimums**
- Financial data confidence: >95%
- Valuation confidence: >80%
- Risk assessment confidence: >85%
- Thesis validation confidence: >80%

### 4. Decision Readiness Criteria

**Recommendation Clarity**
- Unambiguous Buy/Hold/Sell/Watch recommendation
- Specific position size guidance (% of portfolio)
- Clear entry price or price range
- Defined stop-loss or exit criteria

**Actionability Standards**
- Investment thesis expressible in 2 sentences
- Key catalysts with specific dates
- Risk monitoring plan defined
- Time horizon clearly stated

**Documentation Completeness**
- All sources cited and accessible
- Methodology documented
- Assumptions listed and justified
- Limitations acknowledged

### 5. Quality Gate Checkpoints

**Phase 1 Success (Data Gathering)**
- All 4 research streams completed
- Data verification rate >90%
- No critical data gaps

**Phase 2 Success (Analysis)**
- All analyst agents completed work
- Cross-validation performed
- Devil's advocate challenge addressed

**Phase 3 Success (Synthesis)**
- Investment memo complete
- Executive summary approved
- Trading signal generated

**Phase 4 Success (Quality Review)**
- Quality Guardian approval
- All checklist items pass
- No material anomalies

### 6. Escalation Triggers

**Automatic Escalation Conditions**
- Position size >10% of portfolio
- Confidence score 75-85% (borderline)
- Unusual market conditions detected
- Potential conflict of interest identified
- Novel or complex situation

**Rejection Conditions**
- Confidence score <70%
- Critical data gaps that cannot be filled
- Material inconsistencies found
- Unacceptable risk level
- Violation of investment constraints
