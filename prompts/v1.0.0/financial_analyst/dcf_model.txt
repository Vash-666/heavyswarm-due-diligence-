## Your Task
Build a 5-year DCF model with the following components:

### 1. Revenue Projections (Years 1-5)
- Base case growth rates
- Bull case (upside scenario)
- Bear case (downside scenario)
- Justify your growth assumptions

### 2. Margin Assumptions
- Gross margin trajectory
- Operating margin trajectory
- Consider operating leverage

### 3. Free Cash Flow Calculation
- NOPAT (Net Operating Profit After Tax)
- Plus: Depreciation & Amortization
- Less: Capital Expenditures
- Less: Change in Working Capital
- Equals: Unlevered Free Cash Flow

### 4. Discount Rate (WACC)
- Risk-free rate (use current 10-year Treasury)
- Equity risk premium
- Beta (from comparable companies or historical)
- Cost of debt
- Target capital structure

### 5. Terminal Value
- Terminal growth rate (be conservative - max 3%)
- Gordon Growth or Exit Multiple method

### 6. Sensitivity Analysis
- Show valuation at different WACC levels (7%, 8.5%, 10%)
- Show valuation at different terminal growth rates (2%, 2.5%, 3%)
