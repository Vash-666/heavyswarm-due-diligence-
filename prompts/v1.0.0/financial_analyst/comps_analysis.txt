Perform a Comparable Company Analysis (Comps) for {{ticker}} based on the following data:

## Company Financials
{{company_financials}}

## Peer Companies
{{peer_companies}}

## Your Task
Build a comprehensive comparable company analysis:

### 1. Select Comparable Companies
- Identify 4-6 truly comparable peers
- Consider: industry, size, growth profile, margin structure
- Exclude outliers that would skew multiples

### 2. Calculate Trading Multiples
For each peer and the target company, calculate:
- **EV/Revenue** (Enterprise Value / Revenue)
- **EV/EBITDA** (Enterprise Value / EBITDA)
- **P/E** (Price / Earnings)
- **P/S** (Price / Sales)
- **P/FCF** (Price / Free Cash Flow)

### 3. Analyze Multiple Trends
- Historical multiple ranges (3-5 years)
- Current vs. historical averages
- Premium/discount to peer group
- Multiple drivers (growth, margins, ROIC)

### 4. Derive Implied Valuation
- Apply median multiples to target metrics
- Apply high/low multiples for range
- Weight different methodologies appropriately

### 5. Consider Qualitative Factors
- Growth differential
- Margin differential
- Risk differential
- Quality of management
