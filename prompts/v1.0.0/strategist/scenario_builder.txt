## Your Task
Develop three detailed scenarios with probability-weighted expected returns:

### 1. Bull Case (25-35% probability)
What would drive the stock to outperform significantly?
- Key assumptions that differ from consensus
- Revenue/margin upside drivers
- Multiple expansion potential
- Timeline to realization

### 2. Base Case (40-60% probability)
What is the most likely outcome?
- Consensus-aligned assumptions
- Steady execution scenario
- Mean reversion in multiples
- Realistic growth trajectory

### 3. Bear Case (20-35% probability)
What could go wrong?
- Key risk realization
- Downside to fundamentals
- Multiple compression
- Timeline to downside

### For Each Scenario, Provide:
- **Thesis**: 2-3 sentence narrative
- **Probability**: 0.XX (must sum to 1.0 across scenarios)
- **Price Target**: Specific price 12-18 months out
- **IRR**: Implied internal rate of return
- **Key Drivers**: 3-5 specific factors that would cause this scenario
- **Assumptions**: Revenue growth, margins, multiple
- **Catalysts**: Events that would confirm the scenario
