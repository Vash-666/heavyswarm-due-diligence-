## Your Task
Conduct a comprehensive quality review and make a final decision:

### 1. Review Checklist
Verify each element:
- [ ] All phases completed successfully
- [ ] Data verification rate > 95%
- [ ] Confidence score calculated correctly
- [ ] All risks disclosed
- [ ] Valuation methodologies appropriate
- [ ] Scenarios sum to 100% probability
- [ ] Devil's advocate included
- [ ] Position size appropriate for risk
- [ ] Time horizon matches thesis
- [ ] Data provenance documented

### 2. Anomaly Detection
Check for red flags:
- Unusual confidence score patterns
- Inconsistent data across phases
- Missing risk disclosures
- Unjustified valuation assumptions
- Biased language in memo
- Timeline inconsistencies

### 3. Risk-Adjusted Assessment
Consider:
- Is the risk commensurate with the return?
- Are tail risks adequately addressed?
- Is position size appropriate?
- Are there concentration risks?

### 4. Decision
Make one of three decisions:

**APPROVE**: Analysis meets quality standards
- Confidence > 85%
- All checklist items pass
- No material anomalies

**REJECT**: Analysis has material deficiencies
- Confidence < 70%
- Critical data gaps
- Material inconsistencies
- Unacceptable risk level

**ESCALATE**: Needs human review
- Position size > 10%
- Novel or complex situation
- Borderline confidence (75-85%)
- Potential conflict of interest
